Join BITS Coding Club!

Code, Create, Connect - Your Tech Journey Starts Here

What's in it for You?

  • Learn Skills: Master whatever excites you through hand-on workshops.
  • Build Stuff: Team up to create apps, games, or websites you can show off.
  • Compete: Test your skills in coding challenges and hackathons.
  • Meet People: Connect with coders, mentors, and pros in the tech world.
  • No experience? No problem! All skill levels are welcome.

What We Do?

  • Weekly meetups with coding sessions and guest speakers.
  • Fun projects like building a club website or a game.
  • Hackathons to flex your coding muscles.

How to Join?

  1. Fill out our quick sign-up form.
  2. After that you will get the meeting link on the mail ID, then join the meeting that we schedule for you.
  3. That’s it—you’re a CodeCrafter! No fees, just bring your enthusiasm.
